## Blockers

- Real M-Team `/api/torrent/search` responses only exposed summary fields such as `audioCodec`, `videoCodec`, and `hasChineseSubtitle`; full per-track audio/subtitle data was observed under `/api/torrent/detail` as `mediainfo` and under `/api/torrent/mediaInfo` as direct `data` text.
- `/api/torrent/mediaInfo` has no published separate quota. Live probes showed HTTP 200 + `請求過於頻繁` + empty `data` for rate limiting, fixed small intervals down to 1 second can work only for limited batches, long-run probes failed at request 25 with 1-second spacing, request 49 with 2-second spacing, request 50 with 3-second spacing, and request 25 with 5-second spacing, zero-delay burst thresholds are stateful and inconsistent, and recovery can exceed 10 minutes after stronger/repeated limit events. A later 1-second cycle/backoff probe reached 100 total requests with failures at totals 51 and 77 and successful 5-minute recovery checks after both. Follow-up enrichment should cache successful responses, stay single-concurrency, avoid large short-interval batches, and use long backoff after rate-limit messages.
- A 2026-07-08 `/api/torrent/mediaInfo` 200-success cycle/backoff probe needed 209 total requests and saw 9 rate-limit responses. With 1-second active spacing, the first three active cycles each failed on attempt 51; the first two limit events recovered after a 5-minute quiet check, while the third remained limited at 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, and 10 minutes and recovered at 11 minutes.
- A 2026-07-08 `/api/torrent/mediaInfo` 360-success cycle/backoff probe with 10-second active spacing needed 370 total requests and saw 10 rate-limit responses. The first active cycle failed on attempt 101 and the second on attempt 151; both events recovered on the 9-minute backoff check after 5-, 6-, 7-, and 8-minute checks failed.
- A 2026-07-08 cross-endpoint probe showed `/api/torrent/detail` still returned `SUCCESS` with `mediainfo` immediately after `/api/torrent/mediaInfo` returned `請求過於頻繁` on the 51st 1-second-spaced attempt, suggesting the observed MediaInfo limiter is not an immediate whole-token block for detail.
- A 2026-07-08 `/api/torrent/detail` probe showed detail also has a `請求過於頻繁` state: baseline succeeded, 1-second interval attempts 1-49 succeeded, and attempt 50 returned the rate-limit message. Immediately after the detail limit event, `/api/torrent/mediaInfo` still returned `SUCCESS`, suggesting `detail` and `mediaInfo` have separate immediate limiter buckets even though a broader global quota remains possible.
- A 2026-07-08 dual-endpoint alternating probe used one request every 10 seconds across `/api/torrent/mediaInfo` and `/api/torrent/detail`, giving each endpoint a 20-second minimum interval and applying 12-minute cooldown only to the endpoint that returned `請求過於頻繁`. It reached 500 successful responses after 506 total requests: each endpoint produced 250 successes and 2 rate-limit responses, with 2 transient HTTP 502 transport errors. This supports endpoint-specific cooldown as a higher-throughput strategy than stopping all enrichment when only one endpoint is limited.
- A 2026-07-08 same-account token-switch probe showed switching API tokens did not bypass `/api/torrent/mediaInfo` limiting. Both tokens succeeded at baseline; token A hit `請求過於頻繁` on the 49th 1-second-spaced trigger attempt, and an immediate token B request also returned `請求過於頻繁`. This rules out a simple per-token-only limiter in that case, though it cannot distinguish account-level from IP-level limiting.
